# Design Brief

**Purpose & Tone:** Premium artisanal fresh fish and masala e-commerce. Celebrates freshness, quality, careful curation. Confident yet approachable — not corporate, not rustic.

**Visual Direction:** Fresh Minimalism — seafoam teal (ocean-inspired, fresh) paired with warm sand neutrals. Natural green accent for herbs/vitality. Clean typography hierarchy via serif display + sans body. Photo-forward design with large product imagery.

**Typography:** Fraunces (display, serif) for headlines — elegant, high-end food feel. DM Sans (body, sans-serif) for content — clean, professional, readable. Type scale: 5xl/4xl/3xl display → lg/sm body.

**Shape Language:** Border radius 8–12px on cards for approachability without playfulness. Minimal shadows — rely on elevation via color and subtle borders.

| Zone | Background | Treatment | Purpose |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Header | White/Card | Bottom border (border color) | Navigation, logo, cart. Clean separation. |
| Hero/Featured | Secondary (Sand) | Teal accent bar or overlay | Large product imagery, featured category. |
| Product Grid | Background (Light) | White cards (card color) with subtle shadows | Product showcase, filterable grid. |
| Footer | Secondary (Sand) | Minimal, open layout | Links, contact, social. Mirrors header tone. |
| Sections | Background | Alternate muted/40 every other row | Visual rhythm, breathing room. |

**Component Patterns:** Card-based layout for products. Minimal hover states (slight lift, color shift). CTA buttons teal with white text. Secondary buttons have sand background with teal text. Destructive actions have red background.

**Motion:** Smooth transitions (0.3s cubic-bezier) on interactive elements. No bounce or ease-out dominance. Subtle scale on hover for product cards.

**Differentiation:** Photo-forward design. Every product has large, high-quality imagery. Serif typography pairing elevates e-commerce standard. Teal + sand + green palette is distinctive yet calm — no purple/gradient noise.

**Constraints:** No full-page gradients. No bounce animations. Keep spacing generous (breathing room). Typography hierarchy drives visual weight, not color alone.

**Signature Detail:** Teal accent bar on featured product sections or category headers — signals freshness and premium positioning.
